International Magical Incident — Urgent Report

Dobby the House‑Elf Discovered on Indonesia’s South Sea Coast

Muggle witnesses report unusual magical phenomena

HOGWARTS, SCOTLAND — In a shocking international development, the body of Dobby, the free house‑elf and known ally of Harry Potter, has reportedly been discovered on Indonesia’s south coast. The circumstances of his death are under active investigation by wizarding authorities.

Discovery

The discovery was made by a group of Indonesian secondary‑school girls during a coastal excursion. Witnesses described finding a small humanoid creature near the tide line; authorities were notified immediately.

Transported Across Continents

Sources within the Ministry of Magic suggest that Dobby may have been forcibly transported thousands of miles from Britain amid escalating hostile activities. One senior official from the Department of International Magical Cooperation stated:

“Such a displacement would require extraordinary power. It is deeply concerning that this incident occurred outside British jurisdiction.”

Breach of Secrecy in Indonesia

The situation became particularly delicate after reports surfaced that the Muggle students researched their discovery online. The Indonesian Ministry of Magic (known locally as Kementerian Sihir Nusantara) is working urgently with British authorities to contain the breach. Obliviators have reportedly attended to witnesses where practicable.

A confidential report suggests that one of the girls was overheard saying:

“If this is real… then Hogwarts is real too.”

International Response

Hogwarts staff have declined to comment publicly, though students confirm that a memorial has been established near the kitchens, where Dobby once worked. Candlelight tributes have also been reported along Indonesia’s South Sea coast, where local magical communities are quietly honouring Dobby’s bravery.

Though the Statute of Secrecy remains officially intact, this unprecedented cross‑continental incident has raised concerns among wizarding authorities worldwide.

Dobby died free. And in death, he may have bridged two worlds.

The Hogwarts Herald will continue monitoring this developing story.

International Magical Investigation

Nimbus 2000 Discovered Hovering Above Indonesia’s South Sea

SOUTH SEA COAST, INDONESIA — In a development as astonishing as the discovery of Dobby, a second magical anomaly was confirmed along the shores: a broomstick identified as a Nimbus 2000 was seen hovering above the water before vanishing.

On the evening of the incident, several beachgoers reported initially seeing what they believed to be “a stick suspended by invisible string.” As dusk settled, the object took shape and was later identified through residual enchantment tracing as a Nimbus 2000, a broom model famously flown at Hogwarts.

Seven Minutes of Silence

According to eyewitness accounts, the broom remained suspended for precisely seven minutes. It neither advanced nor retreated, but hovered as if awaiting instruction. Moments before its disappearance, the broom reportedly tilted forward — the unmistakable posture of a racing launch — and shot upwards into the darkening sky, vanishing without sound.

When representatives from Kementerian Sihir Nusantara arrived, they discovered concentrated traces of flight‑magic and transcontinental displacement energy identical to that detected at the Dobby scene.

A Message — or a Malfunction?

Scholars are divided on the purpose of the manifestation. Some theorise the broom may have been caught in a disrupted Portkey current linking Britain and Indonesia. Others suggest something far more deliberate — that the broom’s appearance was a signal or a warning.

“Nimbus brooms do not simply wander across oceans. If this was intentional, it implies coordination — or interference — at a very high magical level,”

a senior British Ministry official commented cautiously.

The Maitreyawira Alignment

Investigators mapping magical residue across Palembang have uncovered a curious alignment: the coordinates of the broom’s hovering location correspond almost precisely with ley‑line intersections in the region. Though no formal declaration has been made, whispers within the Department of Mysteries suggest that Maitreyawira may sit upon a dormant conduit — one possibly resonant with Hogwarts’ own ancient networks.

Students in the surrounding area have reportedly experienced brief electrical disruptions and fleeting shadows resembling winged shapes overhead. While such claims remain unverified, authorities are treating them with caution.

Echoes Across the Sea

The Nimbus 2000’s appearance marks the second confirmed magical occurrence on Indonesia’s southern coast within a fortnight. Combined with the earlier tragedy involving Dobby, speculation is mounting about a wider pattern.

For now, both British and Indonesian magical officials maintain that the International Statute of Secrecy remains secure. Obliviators have attended to the necessary witnesses, and patrol charms have been strengthened around key nodes.

If a broom can cross continents unaided… what else might follow?

The Hogwarts Herald continues its investigation into the growing connection between Indonesia’s South Sea and the hidden corridors of Hogwarts.

Forest Omen

Centaur Apparition in Indonesian Rainforest Deepens Mystery

SUMATRA, INDONESIA — As investigations into the South Sea anomalies continue, a third extraordinary incident emerged — this time deep within the rainforest rather than by the shore. Local villagers reported sighting a towering figure at twilight: the upper body of a man with the powerful frame of a horse below. The being stood in a forest clearing beneath a rising crescent moon.

Magical experts confirmed the description matches that of a Centaur, a sentient magical being traditionally residing within the Forbidden Forest near Hogwarts. Centaurs are not known to travel casually, nor do they interfere with wizarding affairs without purpose.

A Scholar of the Skies

Renowned for their mastery of astronomy and interpretation of celestial signs, Centaurs observe the heavens as closely as scholars study books. One witness claimed the creature traced patterns in the soil with the tip of a carved wooden spear before retreating into the mist without sound. No signs of aggression were recorded.

Officials from Kementerian Sihir Nusantara detected faint but unmistakable traces of British forest‑magic signature — the same resonance pattern linked to the other recent events.

Celestial Alignment

Astronomical records reveal that on the night of the sighting, several constellations traditionally studied by Centaur clans were in rare alignment. A Hogwarts professor of Divination, speaking anonymously, remarked:

“Centaurs read the heavens as we read books. If one has journeyed so far from Britain, the sky must have written something urgent indeed.”

When mapped geographically, the rainforest clearing lies along the same ley‑line trajectory connecting the South Sea coastline and Maitreyawira School in Palembang. This marks the third magical manifestation in Indonesia within a short span:

  • The displacement of Dobby.
  • The hovering Nimbus 2000.
  • A Centaur sighting beneath Sumatra’s stars.

Scholars now speculate whether an ancient conduit between Southeast Asia and Hogwarts has reawakened — possibly through cosmic alignment rather than deliberate spellwork.

Artefact Anomaly

The Marauder’s Map Surfaces in Indonesia — Then Vanishes Without Trace

SOUTH SEA COAST, INDONESIA — In what may prove to be the most alarming development yet, an enchanted parchment resembling the legendary Marauder’s Map was reportedly found at low tide by early‑morning visitors and then vanished moments later.

At first glance, the parchment appeared ordinary and weathered. Witnesses described ink lines shifting across it and faint words appearing briefly across the top before fading. Moments after a finder touched the document, the parchment vanished entirely — leaving no ash or residue, only slightly warm air and disturbed sand.

Instantaneous Disappearance

Magical investigators confirmed traces of concealment enchantments consistent with defensive spells designed to protect sensitive artefacts. If authentic, the Marauder’s Map is no ordinary object. Created by Messrs Moony, Wormtail, Padfoot and Prongs, the map reveals hidden passages and the movements of those within Hogwarts.

A Deliberate Warning?

The Department of Mysteries has reportedly taken interest in the timing of the event. The map’s appearance occurred nine days after the Nimbus 2000 sighting and under similar tidal conditions, suggesting pattern rather than coincidence. An unnamed British official observed:

“The Map does not simply drift across oceans. It responds to intent. If it appeared, someone — or something — meant it to be seen.”

The Maitreyawira Hypothesis Strengthens

All three manifestations — the Nimbus 2000, the Centaur sighting, and the Marauder’s Map — align geographically with subterranean magical currents beneath Maitreyawira School in Palembang. Advanced rune analysis conducted jointly by British and Indonesian authorities indicates the school grounds may rest upon an ancient ley‑line convergence point, potentially synchronised with distant places of power.

Some scholars cautiously propose the existence of a dormant “Mirror Gate” — a magical resonance phenomenon in which two distant locations share structural enchantment patterns, allowing objects or beings to appear across vast distances. No official confirmation has been issued.

Four Incidents. One Convergence.

  • Dobby’s tragic displacement to Indonesia’s South Sea.
  • The hovering Nimbus 2000.
  • A silent Centaur beneath Sumatra’s stars.
  • The fleeting reappearance of the Marauder’s Map.

Each event grows less accidental than the last. Whether these manifestations represent a warning, a fracture in magical boundaries, or the awakening of a long‑forgotten alliance between Hogwarts and a distant institution remains uncertain.

Indonesia’s South Sea is no longer merely a shoreline — it has become a threshold.

The Hogwarts Herald will continue its coverage as authorities on two continents seek answers hidden between tide and starlight.
